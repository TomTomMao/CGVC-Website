Accomodation

CGVC 2018 is co-located with DiRAC Science Day (http://pyweb.swan.ac.uk/diracday). DiRAC has set up special accommodation for their delegates for a discounted rate of only Â£30/night. CGVC delegates can visit the DiRAC day web page, http://pyweb.swan.ac.uk/diracday, and click on the button to go to registration and accommodation. Delegates can then register for DiRAC Science Day (for free) and are very welcome to attend the DiRAC day talks/posters on 12 Sept (and enjoy the catering on offer). However, do not sign up to present a poster unless you have used DiRAC HPC facilities to get results.
