Registration Fee

  • Academic/industry(non member): £175
  • Academic/industry/retired (member of Eurographics): £99
  • Retired (non member): £99
  • Student (member and non member of Eurographics): £99
  • Attendance only: Free

Academics, people from the industry have to be members of the Eurographics. Non members can choose Option 1, which include the corresponding EG Membership. Membership for students is optional, but encouraged.
