--- layout: default title: CGVC 2020 Registration --- We’re pleased to announce that the registration for CGVC 2020 is now open. Given the COVID-19 situation we currently live in, the conference will be held online via video conferencing. Attendance at the virtual conference will be free, the costs being covered by the Eurographics association and our sponsors. We need to ask that attendees however complete the registration to ensure capacity can be managed properly on the online platform. Start Registration

Prices

  • Student (member and non member of Eurographics): £99
  • Academic/industry/retired (member of Eurographics): £99
  • Academic/industry(non member): £175
  • Retired (non member): £99

Academics, people from the industry have to be members of the Eurographics. Non members can choose Option 3, which include the corresponding EG Membership. Membership for students is optional, but encouraged.
