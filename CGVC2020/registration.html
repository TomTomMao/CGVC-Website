--- layout: default title: CGVC 2020 Registration --- We’re pleased to announce that the registration for CGVC 2020 is now open.

Prices

  • Student (member and non member of Eurographics): £125
  • Academic/industry/retired (member of Eurographics): £175
  • Academic/industry (non member): £247
  • Retired (non member): £225
  • Additional CGVC 2020 Dinner tickets: £30
  • Optional Eurographics membership for students: £41

Academics, people from the industry, and retired people have to be members of the Eurographics. Non members can choose Options 3 or 4, which include the corresponding EG Membership. Membership for students is optional, but encouraged.
